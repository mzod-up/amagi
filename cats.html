There are a million reasons why cats are awesome

But here are 5:

1. They're cool little guys.

They just look cool. Check out Uni. That perfect mouth shape. *Chef's kiss*

2. They're independent.

They like to go out on their own adventures sometimes, so you don't really have to worry if your cat goes missing for a little while. Besides, they're natural hunters with great survival skills.

3. They're thoughtful.

Sometimes they leave you presents (and who doesn't like presents!) including but not limited to:

  • Mice
  • Lizards
  • Birds
  • Your neighbor's favorite socks

Mine gave me a squid once. Don't know how he got a hold of that.

4. They all have different personalities.

Just like people do. Not all of them are mean or feisty like in the movies.
Here are some stats for cats I've gotten to know personally:

Personality Cat Count
Super Friendly 4
Pretty Aloof 2
Literally Hates People 1, yes Matrix this is about you

5. They're silly.

They're super curious and sometimes it makes them do silly things.
There are lots of videos of cats being silly. YouTube loves them.

Show cats a little more love. Boop that snoot.
